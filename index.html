AboutUs

During the summer of 2013 Graham worked in the scene shop and Kayla worked in the admin offices for the Williamstown Theatre Festival. Thus began a slow orbit of each other that ended in - of all things - an OKCupid match. On December 31, 2016, Graham and Kayla swiped right. Taylor (the officiant) still assumes partial credit for hyping Kayla up. A week or so later, the two had an hours-long first date at the Harlem Public (which they still live next door to), much of which was spent reminiscing about the summer they didn't really cross paths. Kayla loves to remind Graham to this day that he was "one of the cool kids" that summer.

By April 2017, the two were nearly inseperable, and in May, the two traveled together for the first time, which sparked their love for traveling with each other. So far, they've been to Dublin, Ireland, Colorado, Connecticut, DC, Florida, Georgia, New Hampshire, North Carolina, Maryland, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, South Carolina, Vermont, and hopefully more to come!

On January 8th, 2021, Graham asked Kayla to go on a walk to her favorite local park. As they walked, he explained an elaborate plan that had started in early 2020, which included international travel and an elaborate proposal. Although COVID had up-ended their lives (and the entire world), it hadn't changed how they felt about each other, and four years later, Graham asked Kayla to marry him. Although Kayla was eager to get married as soon as possible, Graham (correctly) suggested a longer engagement to be able to enjoy the process of wedding planning. So that's our story. We can't wait to see you on May 27, 2023!
